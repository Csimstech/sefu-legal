# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** April 24, 2026
**Effective Date:** April 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how SeFu ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our mobile application (the "App"), operated by CSimsTech.

By using SeFu, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

- **Account information:** Email address (if you create an account with email/password)
- **Profile information:** Age range, profession, focus areas, interests, goals, difficulty preference, and time commitment — provided during onboarding to personalize your weekly challenges
- **Reflections:** Any text you write when reflecting on completed challenges

### Information Collected Automatically

- **Usage data:** Challenge assignments, completions, swaps, streaks, and progression metrics
- **Device information:** Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers
- **Subscription data:** Subscription tier, billing status, and product identifiers (managed via Apple App Store and Google Play Store via RevenueCat)
- **Diagnostic data:** Crash reports and performance data (via Firebase Crashlytics)

### Information We Do NOT Collect

- We do not collect your real name (unless you provide it)
- We do not collect your physical address
- We do not collect your phone number
- We do not access your contacts, photos, microphone, or location
- We do not track you across other apps or websites
- We do not collect biometric data

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- Personalize your weekly challenge assignments based on your profile
- Track your progress, streaks, and growth over time
- Send notifications about your weekly challenge (if you opt in)
- Process subscriptions and manage premium access
- Improve the App through anonymized usage analytics
- Diagnose and fix bugs
- Communicate with you about important changes to the App or your account

---

## 3. How We Share Your Information

We do **not** sell your personal information. Ever.

We share information only with the following service providers, who help us run the App:

- **Firebase (Google Cloud):** Hosts our backend infrastructure, including authentication, database, and notifications
- **RevenueCat:** Processes subscription purchases and manages billing
- **Apple App Store / Google Play Store:** Process payments per their respective privacy policies
- **Vercel:** Hosts our admin dashboard (operator-only access)

These services are bound by their own privacy policies and may not use your data for their own purposes.

We may also disclose information when required by law (e.g., subpoena) or to protect our rights and the safety of our users.

---

## 4. Your Rights and Choices

### Access and Update

You can view and update your profile information at any time via the **Profile** page in the App.

### Delete Your Account

You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time:

- **In-app:** Settings → Account → Delete my account
- **By request:** Email support@sefu.app from the email associated with your account

We will delete your account within 7 days of receiving the request. See [Account Deletion](delete-account) for details on what data is removed.

### Notifications

You can disable push notifications at any time via your device settings or the App's notification preferences.

### Data Portability

To request a copy of your account data, email support@sefu.app. We will provide your data within 30 days.

---

## 5. Data Retention

- **Active accounts:** We retain your data for as long as your account is active
- **Deleted accounts:** Your profile, progress, and personal data are permanently deleted within 7 days
- **Billing records:** We may retain subscription/billing records for up to 7 years to comply with tax and legal obligations
- **Anonymized usage data:** May be retained indefinitely for analytics, but cannot be linked back to you

---

## 6. Data Security

We use industry-standard security practices, including:

- HTTPS/TLS encryption for all data in transit
- Encryption at rest in Firebase
- Limited employee access to user data
- Regular security audits

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

---

## 7. Children's Privacy

SeFu is intended for users 18 years of age and older. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has used SeFu, please email support@sefu.app and we will delete the account.

---

## 8. International Users

SeFu is operated from the United States. By using SeFu, you consent to the transfer and processing of your data in the United States.

If you are in the European Economic Area (EEA) or United Kingdom, you have additional rights under GDPR including the right to access, correct, and delete your data, and to object to certain processing. To exercise these rights, email support@sefu.app.

If you are in California, you have additional rights under the CCPA. We do not sell your personal information.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you in the App and update the "Last Updated" date above.
