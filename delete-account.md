# Delete Your SeFu Account

This page explains how to permanently delete your SeFu account and all associated data.

**Operated by:** CSimsTech
**Contact:** support@sefu.app

---

## How to Delete Your Account

### Option 1: Delete from inside the app (recommended)

1. Open the SeFu app
2. Tap the menu icon (☰) in the top-left
3. Tap **Settings**
4. Scroll to the **Account** section
5. Tap **Delete my account**
6. Confirm your decision

Your account and all data will be permanently deleted within 7 days.

### Option 2: Email request

If you cannot access the app:

1. Email **support@sefu.app** from the email address associated with your account
2. Subject line: **Delete my account**
3. Include any identifying information (such as the email you used to sign up)

We will verify your identity and delete your account within 7 days of receiving the request.

---

## What Data Is Deleted

When you delete your account, we permanently remove:

- Your user profile (age range, profession, focus areas, interests, goals, preferences)
- Your weekly challenge assignments and history
- Your bonus challenges and daily micro-challenge records
- Your reflections and notes
- Your progress data (streaks, levels, completion records)
- Your notification preferences
- Your account credentials

---

## What Data May Be Retained

We may retain limited records for legal and accounting purposes:

- **Billing records:** Subscription transaction history may be retained for up to 7 years to comply with US tax obligations
- **Anonymized usage statistics:** May be kept indefinitely but cannot be linked back to you

This retention is required by law and is not used to identify or track you.

---

## Subscription Cancellation

Deleting your account does NOT automatically cancel your subscription.

To cancel an active subscription:

- **iPhone:** Settings app → tap your name → Subscriptions → SeFu → Cancel
- **Android:** Google Play Store → tap profile icon → Payments and subscriptions → Subscriptions → SeFu → Cancel

You should cancel your subscription **before** deleting your account to avoid being charged after deletion.
